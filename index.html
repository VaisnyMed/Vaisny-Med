ABOUT VAISNY BALAMURALI

Hailing from Toronto, Canada I am a passionate & results-driven healthcare research professional and medical student (MS3). My interests lie in applying analytical skills to interpret clinical data to inform health systems planning and clinical care delivery; improving the quality & sustainability of health care delivery and ultimately improving population health outcomes & statuses. My non-traditional journey to become a physician was driven by my desire to leverage frontline experience as a clinician to advocate for improved & equitable healthcare access and patient care outcomes within the realms of women’s health and marginalized populations.
